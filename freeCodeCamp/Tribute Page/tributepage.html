Khalid bin al-Walid

The Sword of Allah

Here a story of Khalid al-Walid:

  • 7th century – An Arab Muslim commander
  • 625 – Khalid played an instrumental role in defeating Muhammad SAW and his followers during the Battle of Uhud
  • 627 / 629 – he converted to Islam in the presence of Muhammad SAW, who inducted him as an official military commander among the Muslims and gave him the title of Sayf Allah (Sword of Allah)
  • 629–630 – The Battle of Hunayn
  • 630 – He led the Bedouins under the Muslim army during the Muslim conquest of Mecca
  • 632–633 – He played the leading role in the Ridda Wars against rebel tribes in Arabia
  • 633–634 – The initial campaigns in Sasanian Iraq
  • 634–638 – The conquest of Byzantine Syria
  • 642 – Khalid died in either Medina or Homs

"I die in shame, I die as a camel dies, may the eyes of a coward never close even in sleep."
